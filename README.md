🌍 CarbonWise AI – Your Personal Climate Companion

CarbonWise AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform that empowers individuals to understand, monitor, predict, and reduce their carbon footprint through intelligent, personalized, and actionable insights.

🚀 Features

- 🌱 Real-time Carbon Footprint Calculator
- 🤖 AI Climate Coach for personalized recommendations
- 🔍 AI-Powered OCR Analysis for electricity bills and receipts
- 📊 Dynamic Sustainability Score (0–100)
- 💰 Monthly Carbon Budget Tracking
- 🔮 AI Carbon Twin (Digital Twin Technology)
- 📈 Future Impact Simulator (1, 5 & 10 years)
- 🏆 Gamification with badges, streaks, and leaderboards
- 🌍 Geo-Aware Sustainability Recommendations
- 🛡️ Responsible AI with privacy and transparency

🧠 Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- Chart.js
- Font Awesome / Lucide Icons
- AI Recommendation Engine
- OCR Analysis Engine

✨ Key Innovations

AI Carbon Twin

Creates a digital replica of user lifestyle and simulates future environmental impact.

Explainable AI

Every recommendation includes:

- Reason for suggestion
- Estimated CO₂ reduction
- Effort level
- Scientific reasoning

Behavioral Nudging Engine

Encourages sustainable habits through:

- Streaks
- Micro-challenges
- Goal reminders
- Positive reinforcement
